About Us

The Indo-American Seniors Organization of New Jersey is a non-profit community organization that aims to enhance the quality of life of seniors by providing various cultural, educational, and recreational activities. We focus on promoting senior welfare and fostering an inclusive community where members can actively participate in cultural exchange and community service.

The President and executive committee of the Indo American Seniors Organization of New Jersey, welcomes you to have a look at our website and invites you to be a part of our statewide seniors' bigger family.
