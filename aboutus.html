Formation

The Indo American Seniors Organization of New Jersey (IASO of NJ) was formed in the year 2003. IASONJ is located at 665, Lincoln Hwy (RT 27), Iselin, NJ 08830.

Objectives

Our organization is a large family of seniors. Our goals are to provide information related to health, immigration, citizenship, social welfare, and more. We also host social activities, cultural programs, temple visits, local and international tours, and other engaging events.

Activities

We organize various kinds of picnics, tours across the USA and abroad, senior seminars, and workshops. We celebrate national and religious festivals, host lectures by VIPs and leaders, and coordinate with government officers to ensure seniors receive the benefits they are entitled to.

Suggestions

We welcome suggestions, support, and feedback from all members. Every idea is valued, and our Executive Committee works hard to unite the IASONJ community in preserving and sharing our cultural and family values. Our motto: Walk Together, Talk Together, Act with One Mind.
